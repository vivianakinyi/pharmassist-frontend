Reasons to use this app

Saves you time

You need medicine but you are not sure which of the pharmacies you see are licensed and are selling authentic drug? Then, Pharmassist is the solution for you. It saves you time searching for a pharmacy.

Easy to use

Build with you in mind! Pharmassit is easy to use. Simply search for the drug you need, allow the system to acces your location and choose the radius within which you want the results and voila!

Saves you money

The price of drugs vary depending on a pharmacy.With Pharmassit, you can save that extra coin when buying drugs by comparing the price of the same drug in different pharmacies.

Saves you from buying counterfeit drugs

We ensure we display only pharmacies that are selling authentic drugs that have been approved and registerd in RxNorm. Hence when you use Pharmssit you can be sure that all the pharmacies you see selling authentic drugs.
