About Us

PharmAssit is a pharmaceutical drug information system that uses spatial query analysis to determine location of nearest pharmacies with genuine drugs and are selling drugs within the recommended drug price range.

With Pharmasssit you can search for any prescribed drug to get the pharmacies nearest to you with the drug that is licenced and has genuine and authentic drugs. You also get the recommnded price of the drug. In addition, you can get the exact location of the pharmacy or view a full map with the pharmacies!
